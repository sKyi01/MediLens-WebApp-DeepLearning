Pneumonia Diagnostic

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. The air sacs may fill with fluid or pus (purulent material), causing cough with phlegm or pus, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing. A variety of organisms, including bacteria, viruses and fungi, can cause pneumonia.

Skin Cancer Diagnostic

Skin cancer is a malignant condition that originates in the skin cells. It typically develops as a result of the uncontrolled growth of damaged skin cells, often due to prolonged exposure to ultraviolet (UV) radiation from the sun or tanning beds. There are three main types of skin cancer: basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and melanoma.

Tools & Technologies

We are using machine learning algorithms like Logistic Regression, Decision Tree Classifier, Random Forests, as well as Artificial Neural Networks in order to train our model along with the ML libraries such as scikit-learn, numpy, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn & Tensorflow.

MachineLearning

For image processing, we are using convolutional neural networks (CNN) along with the Inception model to predict the best result. We are training over about 5k-10k image dataset with model accuracy more than 85% each. We are using scikit-learn, Tensorflow & Keras for models.
